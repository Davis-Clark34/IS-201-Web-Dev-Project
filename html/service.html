The Reason For the Season


- The Christmas season is upon us. The air is getting colder and Christmas lights are slowly coming out. Generally, this season of the year is full of get-togethers and family time as well as giving gifts to those closest around us. Often, children write their lists of wants and needs and send them to the North Pole for old St. Nick to read so that Santa Claus will bring them gifts. Many people go around "spreading the message of Christmas". Well what is the message of Christmas? As seen above is a depiction of the Nativity scene of the birth of Christ. Simply put, the message of Christmas is that a Savior is born, that Jesus Christ came to this Earth, a God who came in the form of man to live the greatest and purest life that any man could or ever will. Jesus Christ is the center of Christmas so we should strive to emulate him as much as possible. The Christmas spirit is showing Christ-like charity and helping any ad all around us to feel the Love of God. That is what Christmas is about to me. So how can we have the best Christmas??
